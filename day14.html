How This Traffic Light Works

Red Light

Girl signals to stop. Red light stays active for 4 seconds.

Yellow Light

Boy signals to prepare. Yellow flashes for 1 second during transitions.

Green Light

Boy and girl together signal to go. Green stays active for 4 seconds.
